This platform allows users to simulate stock trading and learn market strategies.

Main Features

Create account and login securely.
Buy and sell stocks in a simulated environment.
View your personal portfolio and investment value.
Track all your trading transactions.
View analytics dashboard with stock charts and data.
Upload and update your profile photo.

How to Use the Platform

Step 1: Create your account and login to the dashboard.
Step 2: Browse available stocks on the trader dashboard.
Step 3: Click BUY or SELL to simulate trading.
Step 4: Monitor your portfolio and transaction history.
Step 5: Use analytics charts to analyze stock performance.
