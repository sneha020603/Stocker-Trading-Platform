Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Stocker trading platform.

1. How do I create an account?

Click the Signup button and provide username, email, password and profile photo.

2. How do I buy stocks?

Login → Trader Dashboard → Click Buy next to a stock.

3. How do I sell stocks?

Login → Trader Dashboard → Click Sell.

4. How do I check my portfolio?

Click the Portfolio button on the Trader Dashboard.

5. How do I update my profile photo?

Click the Update Photo button in the navbar.

6. Why are charts showing low numbers?

This happens when few transactions exist. As trades increase, analytics charts update automatically.

7. Who can delete traders?

Only Admin users can delete trader accounts.

8. What analytics are available?

Buy vs Sell trades
Trades per stock
Daily transactions
Top traders
